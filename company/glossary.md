# Glossary

Domain terms used across Tashilcar. Persian term in parentheses where relevant.
Entries marked _(confirm)_ are assumptions to be validated with the team.

## Core concepts
- **Car financing with loan** — buying a car in installments funded by a loan.
- **Installment (قسط)** — a scheduled repayment the customer pays over time.
- **Installment plan (طرح اقساط)** — the full schedule: down payment, count, amount, period. _(confirm exact fields)_
- **Down payment (پیش‌پرداخت)** — initial amount the customer pays up front. _(confirm)_
- **Loan (وام / تسهیلات)** — the financing provided to fund the purchase. "تسهیلات" is the root of the company name.
- **Deal (معامله)** — a transaction is created when a purchase request is approved on the Ad.
- **Application (سفارش)** — this is a loan application for installment purchases, and is made from Deal + Ad.
- **Tashim code (کد تسهیم)** — A template outlining the distribution mechanism for the loan shares received from the order among our company, the Showroom, and the vehicle owner.
- **Contract (قرارداد)** — A contract is created when the application is completed and the customer receives the loan.
- **Sales model (مدل فروش)** — The sales methods we currently have, such as sales through advertisements, pre-sales, and sales of small products (non-automotive).
- **Collections Department (واحد وصول و مطالبات)** — A unit that is directly responsible for monitoring and following up on the repayment status of customer loans.
- **Collections Specialist (ادمین وصول و مطالبات)** — There are people who work in the Collections Department and monitor the status of loan repayments.
- **Letter of Demand (اظهارنامه‌)** — It is an official letter sent by the company, warning the customer that legal action will be taken if they continue to fail to pay their installments.
- **Expense (هزینه)** — These are unexpected additional costs that admins are forced to add to the customer's application to pay.
- **Catalogue (کاتالوگ)** — A catalog includes the make, model, and specifications of a car, and showrooms can only advertise and sell cars for which they have a catalog. It is actually to control the car models that can be sold.
- **Factory Inventory (موجودی کارخانه‌ای)** — An inventory includes the brand, model, and specifications of a car, and showrooms can only pre-sell and sell cars that they have in stock. It is actually to control the car models that can be sold.
- **Car Inspection (کارشناسی خودرو)** — The most common and standard term for vehicle inspection (covering mechanical health, bodywork, and VIN/provenance verification)‌ (Required for Ad approval when registering an Ad).
- **Inspection Providers (ارائه دهندگان کارشناسی)** — Companies or services providing vehicle inspection.
- **Loan Type (نوع وام)** — We have different types of loans, each with different interest rates, repayment periods, and loan amounts. These loans are allocated to showrooms based on our agreement so that customers can use that type of loan.


## Actors
- **Customer (مشتری / خریدار)** — individual buyer; uses Peykan and TashilPay.
- **Dealer (تامین کننده / نمایشگاه / کارخانه)** — car dealer; uses Zamyad.
- **Admin (ادمین / کارشناس)** — internal reviewer/verifier; uses Zhina.
- **Car owner (مالک خودرو)** — Someone who wants to sell their car; Not a user of any of the products.

## Products (proper nouns)
- **Peykan (پیکان)** — car dealer ads + installment sales website.
- **TashilPay (تسهیل‌پی)** — customer finance & installment dashboard.
- **Zamyad (زامیاد)** — car dealer ad & inventory dashboard.
- **Zhina (ژینا)** — admin verify/review dashboard.
- **Swiss Army (سوییس آرمی)** — the shared design system. See [design-system/overview.md](../design-system/overview.md).

## Process terms _(confirm with team)_
- **Verification (احراز / بررسی)** — admin review of a purchase request in Zhina.
- **Purchase request (درخواست خرید)** — a customer's request to buy a car in installments.
