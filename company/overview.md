# Tashilcar — Overview

**Tashilcar** is a **car-financing-with-loan** company. We help individual customers buy cars
through **installment sales backed by a loan**, and we connect that flow to car dealers and an
internal review/verification team.

## The model in one line
A customer finds a car, applies to buy it in installments financed by a loan; Tashilcar verifies
and approves the purchase, the dealer provides the car, and the customer repays over time.

## The 4 products (and who they serve)
- **Peykan** — car advertisement + installment sales website for **individual customers** (the buying front door).
- **TashilPay** — financial management & installment dashboard for **customers** (repay, track installments).
- **Zamyad** — advertisement management & inventory dashboard for **car dealers** (list cars, manage stock).
- **Zhina** — comprehensive **admin** dashboard to manage, verify, and review the purchase process.

See [products/_index.md](../products/_index.md) for details on each.

## How they fit together
1. Dealers list cars and inventory in **Zamyad**.
2. Customers browse and start an installment purchase on **Peykan**.
3. Admins verify and review the purchase in **Zhina**.
4. Customers manage and repay their installments in **TashilPay**.

All four share one design system — **Swiss Army** — with per-product color themes.
See [design-system/overview.md](../design-system/overview.md).
