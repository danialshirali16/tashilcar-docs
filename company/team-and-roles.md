# Team & Roles

A small, cross-functional team. Work is coordinated in **Plane** (self-hosted, Community edition).

## People
| Team | Count | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Product Managers | 2 | Own requirements, approve designs, prioritize, run grooming/planning |
| Product Designer | 1 | **You** — design all 4 products in Figma; own design system usage |
| Frontend | 2 | Build UI (Swiss Army on MUI), implement designs |
| Backend | 4 | APIs, business logic, data |
| Testing | 1 | Tester — verifies tasks before review (Testing sub-task per task) |

> One designer covers **all four products** + the Swiss Army design system — a strong reason for the
> small, single-topic docs in this repo (read only what a task needs).

## Teams in the process
- **Product Team** — gatekeeper: approves requirements and designs; assigns to Design.
- **Design Team** — produces and revises designs in Figma.
- **Frontend / Backend** — build sub-tasks **in parallel** during the sprint.
- **Testing** — runs after build; gates the Review meeting.

## Meetings (from the pipeline)
- **Grooming** — prepared designs presented to all teams; technical comments raised.
- **Planning** — final design presented; FE/BE/Testing sub-tasks created.
- **Review** — built output presented to Design + Product for approval → Done.

See the full flow in [design-process.md](../workflows/design-process.md).
